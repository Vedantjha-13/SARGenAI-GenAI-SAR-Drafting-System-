SAR DRAFTING GUIDELINES

1. Base every statement on verifiable case data only.
2. Clearly identify unusual transaction patterns (velocity, structuring, geographic risk, counterparty anomalies).
3. Include exact amounts, dates, and channels where provided.
4. Avoid legal conclusions that are not supported by facts.
5. Use neutral and professional language suitable for regulatory review.
6. Explicitly call out missing data as INSUFFICIENT_INFORMATION.
7. Separate observed facts from analyst interpretation.

MINIMUM SAR SECTIONS

- Subject
- Activity Summary
- Transactional Evidence
- Risk Indicators
- Filing Recommendation
